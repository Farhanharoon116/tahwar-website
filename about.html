About Tahwar.pk

Revolutionizing wedding planning in Pakistan

Our Story

Tahwar.pk was born from a simple observation: planning a wedding in Pakistan shouldn't be so stressful. We watched families spend months calling venues, comparing unclear pricing, dealing with vague availability, and worrying about whether they were making the right choice.

Founded in 2023, we set out to create Pakistan's most trusted wedding marketplace—a platform that brings transparency, convenience, and confidence to one of life's most important celebrations.

Today, we're proud to serve 500+ verified venues across 8 major cities, helping over 15,000 couples find their perfect wedding venue with transparent pricing and verified reviews.

Our Mission

To make wedding planning in Pakistan transparent, convenient, and joyful by connecting families with verified venues and trusted vendors through technology.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted wedding marketplace, serving every city and helping every couple celebrate their special day with confidence and ease.
