Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tahwar.pk make money?

+
Tahwar.pk charges wedding venues a small commission (5-10%) only when a booking is successfully confirmed. For customers, our service is completely free.

Is booking through Tahwar.pk more expensive?

+
No. The prices you see are the same as booking directly. You get complete price transparency and verified reviews at no extra cost.

Which cities does Tahwar.pk cover?

+
We currently operate in Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, and Quetta with 500+ verified venues.
